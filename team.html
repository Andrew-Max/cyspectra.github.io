With 7+ years of experience developing software, Andrew has worked as everything from developer number two at a drone startup to a consultant for one of the five largest U.S. banks and the largest hospital system in Pennsylvania. He is deeply passionate about writing clean, well architected code, and prefers to work full stack.

He spends his free time mountain biking, snowboarding, and traveling around the world and keeps a travel blog at andrew-max.github.io.
